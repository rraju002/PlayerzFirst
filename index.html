Why Us?

PlayerzFirst is a sports media outlet committed to providing our audience with truthful and intelligent coverage on popular sports topics.

Our Mission

Often times, our favorite sport is oversaturated with false narratives perpetuated against players, coaches and orginizations. PlayerzFirst was curated with the intention of giving fans a player's perspective on various topics to create more correspondence between fans and players.

Our Promise to You

As members of the media we take the role of providing our audience with truthful news extremely seriously. It is our promise to the audience to provide you all with accurate and objective sports media coverage!

Why Objectivity?

Perception is reality and for most, media helps fuel one's perception therefore it is the media's responsibility to provide their audience with objective content in order for the reader/viewer to formulate their own opinion rather than being force fed a narrative.

Our Inspiration

With our platform's founder being in touch with sports and understanding the player's perspective is hardly perpetuated in sports media he decided to create a platform in which players felt they were heard.
